About Me

I am a Scottish artist based in Dundee who loves character design and worldbuilding. I initially studied animation at Dundee and Angus College before graduating Duncan of Jordanstone College of Art and Design with a BDes (Hons) in animation.

I specialise in character design, producing illustrations and storyboarding. All of which fit with my passion for worldbuilding and storytelling through comics, animation and film.

I am proficient and have prior experience working in:

  • Adobe Photoshop
  • Adobe Premiere Pro
  • Adobe After Effects
  • Clip Studio Paint
  • TVPaint
  • Toon Boom Storyboard Pro
  • Microsoft Office Suite
  • Autodesk Maya
